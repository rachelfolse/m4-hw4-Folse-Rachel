About

Ariana Grande is an American singer, songwriter, and actress. She began her career in the Broadway musical "13" and proceeded to play Cat Valentine in the hit show Victorious. Soon after, she released her debut album "Yours Truly". She has followed this up with the releases of My Everything, Dangerous Woman, Sweetener, Thank U Next, and Positions. She also has an extensive fragrance line, releasing 13 fragrances total. She plans to make her return to acting in 2024 with the release of Wicked.

Discography

Yours Truly

Yours Truly is a album that showed off her four octave vocal range. Many people compared it to Mariah Carey. It also began her collaboration history with Big Sean and Mac Miller.

My Everything

My Everything is a album that cemented her place as a pop star. The album went atop the Billboard 200 and had 4 top ten singles, Problem, Love Me Harder, Bang Bang, and Break Free.

Dangerous Woman

Dangerous Woman, originally titled "Moonlight", was a whirlwind project about all aspects of romance. It had three hit singles: Dangerous Woman, Into You, and Side to Side.

Sweetener

Sweetener is a pop and trap infused album about love, healing, and light. The album is purposefully upside down, reflecting the upside down period in her life following the events that took place in Manchester.

Thank U Next

Thank U Next is about pain, but also acceptance and understanding of that pain. This album followed the tragic passing of Mac Miller and had two number one singles, Thank U Next and 7 Rings.

Positions

Positions is an album about finding yourself again after difficult times. It had one number one hit on the Billboard Hot 100, Positions.
